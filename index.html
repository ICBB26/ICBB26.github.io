About ICBB

About ICBB

We are pleased to announce the launch of the first edition of the “1st International Conference on Biotechnology and Bioengineering”, held under the dynamic theme: “Advancing Science for a Sustainable Future Through Biotechnology”, which will take place at the École Nationale Supérieure de Biotechnologie in Constantine, Algeria, from December 6 to 8, 2026.

This international conference aims to serve as a true hub of innovation, bringing together visionary scientists, promising young researchers, and pioneering industrial stakeholders who are shaping the biotechnology of tomorrow. From cutting-edge advances in genetic engineering and synthetic biology to transformative breakthroughs in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability, the event will explore a wide range of scientific topics.

Fully aligned with iternational research and development priorities, this conference seeks to contribute to major strategic challenges related to food security, energy security, and public health by highlighting the central role of biotechnology in building a sustainable and resilient future.

    Submission

    Manuscript submission consists of two straps:
    • First, submit an abstract.
    • If the abstract is accepted, then you are invited to submit a short paper.
    To ensure a smooth submission process, please follow the guidelines outlined below.
    Instructions for authors
    We accept abstracts and papers that adhere to the following format:
    • Abstract: provide a concise and structured summary of the paper (150–250 words maximum). The abstract should include the following elements:
      1. Background/Objective: briefly describe the scientific problem and motivation for the study
      2. Methods: summarize the key experimental approach, materials, or computational method used
      3. Results: highlight the principal findings with key quantitative data if available
      4. Conclusion: state the main conclusion and its significance. Avoid citations, abbreviations, and subheadings in the abstract.
    • Paper: use this template.
    Both the abstract and the paper must be submitted via the following link (register and follow the submission steps): submit your abstract here!
    Important Dates
    • Abstract submission deadline: September 7, 2026.
    • Notification of acceptance: September 20, 2026.
    • Full papers submission: October 15, 2026.
